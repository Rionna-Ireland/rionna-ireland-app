## 1. Who we are

Rionna / Pink Connections is a shared racehorse ownership club based in Ireland. This policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have under GDPR.

## 2. What we collect

When you become a member we collect:

- Your name and email address
- Your payment details (processed and stored by Stripe — we never see your card number)
- Your notification preferences and app usage data
- Content you post inside our Circle community

## 3. How we use it

We use this data to:

- Provide you with membership access, race updates, and push notifications
- Process your subscription payments
- Keep the community safe and moderated
- Send you transactional emails (welcome, news notifications you've opted into)

## 4. Your rights

Under GDPR you have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. Email us to exercise any of these rights — contact details are on the Contact page.

## 5. Changes

We'll post any material changes to this policy on this page and, where appropriate, notify you in-app.
