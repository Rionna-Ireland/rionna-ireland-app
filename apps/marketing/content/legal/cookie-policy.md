## What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember things like your login, your language preference, or anonymous usage stats.

## What we use

We keep cookie usage to a minimum:

- **Essential cookies** — keep you signed in and the site working. These can't be turned off.
- **Analytics cookies** — help us understand which pages and features are useful. You can decline these via the banner on your first visit.

## Managing cookies

You can clear cookies at any time from your browser settings. You can also withdraw consent to non-essential cookies via the banner shown on first visit.

## Third parties

Some of our infrastructure partners (Stripe for payments, Vercel for hosting, Circle for the community, Supabase for storage) may set their own cookies on pages that use their services. See their policies for details.
